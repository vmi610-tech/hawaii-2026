Road to Hana 🌿
✅ Black Sand Beach 12:30–3PMFull Day · Leave 6:30 AM
›
📋 Logistics
🗺
Leave Wailea 6:30–7:00 AM sharp
Drive clockwise from Pa'ia (Hwy 36 → 360). Drive back same way — do NOT take rough back road past MM 46. Bring cash — many stops cash only.
🗺 Mile-by-Mile Stops tap to expand
☕Pa'ia → MM 3 · Starting Out2 must-dos›
Pa'ia
Pa'ia Town ⭐ Must
Coffee & walk the town. Paia Fish Market for breakfast — go early. Reset odometer at MM 0.
MM 2
Twin Falls ⭐ Must
Easy hike, great first waterfall for kids. 6–7 falls total — don't linger too long, better ones ahead.
MM 3
Rainbow Eucalyptus Trees
Left side of road — stunning multicolored bark. Don't hop the fence.
💧MM 9–13 · Waterfall Country5 stops · restrooms at MM 12›
MM 10
Garden of Eden Arboretum
$20/car · Beautiful overlooks, food truck, restrooms. Worth it if not behind schedule.
MM 12
Kaumahina State Wayside Free · Restrooms
Usually less crowded. Short nature loops with dramatic Ke'anae Peninsula view.
MM 13
Punalau Falls (Hidden Gem)
Tiny turnout on far side of bridge. Rarely written about — good chance of having it to yourself.
🌿MM 14–17 · Ke'anae PeninsulaAunty Sandy's + swimming›
MM 16
Aunty Sandy's Banana Bread ⭐ Don't Skip
Cash only. Best banana bread on the road. Sandwiches, beverages, snacks too.
MM 17
Ching's Pond 🏊 Swimming
Marvelous pool. Parking extremely limited — 2–3 cars on ocean side ~80 yds before hairpin. Locals cliff dive here.
🌊MM 18–22 · Wailua & More WaterfallsThree Bears + swim stop›
MM 19–20
Upper Waikani Falls (Three Bears) ⭐ Must
Three-tiered, spectacular after rain. Visible from bridge — mauka side. Police actively ticketing No Parking violators here.
MM 22
Pua'a Ka'a State Wayside Restrooms
Two small waterfalls + a spring-fed pool (cold!). Restrooms here.
🍜MM 23–29 · Nahiku & Food StopsMarketplace + waterfall bridges›
MM 25
Makapipi Falls (Bridge Lookdown)
Walk to middle of bridge, ocean side — look straight down. Be very careful; seasonal, dry about half the time.
MM 27
Coconut Glen's 🍦 ⭐ Don't Skip
Legendary coconut milk ice cream — cash/Venmo only. Hours vary; open most days. A true road-to-Hana classic.
MM 29
Nahiku Marketplace 🍽
Half-dozen stands — Island Chef (fish & chips, shrimp), Nahiku Café (coconut cake!). Good snack stop before Hana.
🏖MM 31–34 · Final Push to HanaBlack sand beach + Red Sand Beach›
MM 32
Wai'anapanapa Black Sand Beach ✅ Booked 12:30–3PM
4 entries · $30 total ($10/vehicle + $5/person) · Arrive by 1:00 PM (first 30 min of slot). Volcanic black sand, sea arch, coastal trail.
🎟 View Ticket (QR Code)
MM 34
Hana Town + Red Sand Beach ⭐ Must
Park on Uakea Rd near Hana Maui Hotel. ~10 min on loose red cinders — wear real shoes. Stunning crimson cove.
🏄Past Hana · Koki, Hamoa & PoolsPaved only · MM 34–42›
Near Hana
Koki Beach + Hamoa Beach
Koki: black + white sand, sea arch view. Hamoa: guide's #1 beach on Maui — best bodysurfing. Don't snorkel here.
Hana Town
Braddah Hutts BBQ / Hana Ranch Restaurant 🍽
Braddah Hutts: BBQ pork & chicken, $15–$20, cash only, closed weekends. Hana Ranch: burgers & salads $20–$45.
MM 42
Seven Sacred Pools / Ohe'o Gulch
⚠ Verify NPS access at nps.gov/hale — sometimes closed. Leave Hana by 4:00–4:30 PM to make dinner.
